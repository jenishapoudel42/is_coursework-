Research

A comparison of our website design with other existing websites and the learnings gained from them.

Website Comparison Overview

www.ecosia.org

This website emphasizes sustainability through colors, content, and messaging, which influenced our eco-friendly theme and design choices.

www.amazon.com

Amazon uses a clean homepage layout with clear navigation and product categories, which helps users easily browse different products.

www.daraz.com

Daraz focuses on product listings with filters and categories. This inspired us to include a simple product layout and filtering structure.

Comparison Summary
Similarities
  • Similar layout structure
  • Use of navigation bar at the top
  • Product-focused homepage design
Differences
  • Different color theme
  • Different content arrangement
  • Different focus on sustainability information
What We Improved
  • Simpler and cleaner layout
  • Better focus on eco-friendly products
  • Easy navigation for beginners

Comparison Table

Website Similarities Differences Improvements Made
Amazon Product grid, search bar Too complex UI Simplified layout
Daraz Categories and filters Focused on discounts rather than sustainability Added eco-friendly focus with clean product presentation
Ecosia Clean layout, simple navigation Focuses on search functionality rather than product selling Applied eco-friendly designs

Summary

Through this research, we understood the importance of clean design, simple navigation, and user-friendly layouts. These learnings helped us design our website more effectively.
